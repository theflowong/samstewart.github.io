Viewing $D_N$ as a trigonometric polynomial in complex variable $z$ on the unit circle in $\mathbb{C}$, we have \\[ \begin{align} D_N &= z^{-N} + \cdots + z^{(N - 1)} + z^N \cr &= z^{-N}(1 + \cdots + z^{2N} \cr &= z^{-N}\frac{1 - z^{2N + 1}}{1 - z} \end{align} \\] Multiplying the top and bottom by $-z^{-1/2}$ gives \\[ D_N = \frac{z^{(N + 1/2)} - z^{-(N + 1/2)}}{z^{1/2} - z^{-1/2}} = \frac{2 \sin((2N + 1) \pi x)}{2 \sin(\pi x)}. \\]
